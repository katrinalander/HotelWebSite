This site has been designed for the technical interview by Katrina Lander. The project has been used Bootstrap, AngularJS, HTML, CSS, Java, MySQL.

Site is a sample of the hotel. On the "OUR SUITES" you can see the types of rooms and levels and can create reservation. The page "SEARCH" is presented for finding and booking multiple rooms for groups. Of course all of the reservations held to registered customers, so for registered member sub-menu appears with name of his e-mail. Going to this link a customer can see their current reservations, cancel them or to update his contact details. In any hotel must be "Hot deal" prices, for this I created page "HOT DEAL", here is You can see type of room in deal price, but if this type in amount more than 5 for next 3 days.

And of course, what site without "FEEDBACK", here is customers can leave their comment and put rating for site, and automatically will be send email to admin with name, email, rating and comment. Most operations in site are followed by confirmation email.

All data is processed and entered into the database of MySQL.
